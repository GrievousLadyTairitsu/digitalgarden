# Animal feeding
##### Types of Feeders
- There are four primary types of feeders:
- 1. Filter Feeders
	- Aquatic Organisms (that consume plankton)
	- Water washes through them, and use their unique structures to trap food.
		- Need to ensure that their "filters" don't get damaged
	- Common examples include manta rays, whales etc.
- 2. Substrate Feeders
	- These organisms live on their food source and eat through the same source.
		- ie. (A caterpillar literally eats itself to create a butterfly)
- Fluid Feeder
	- Suck or lick nutrients from other organisms
		- Ie. (Bats, mosquitos, butterflies..)
- Bulk Feeder
	- Ingest large food and either swallow them whole or chew them into smaller pieces
