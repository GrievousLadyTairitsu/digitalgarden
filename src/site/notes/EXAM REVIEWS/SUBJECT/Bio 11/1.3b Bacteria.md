# Bacteria
#### Bacteria Properties
- Contains cell wall (diff from plant wall)
	- Made of peptidoglycan, which is a biological polymer
		- Chain Structure:
	- Provides structure for bacteria
- May be photosynthetic (cyanobacteria) which are autotrophs
#### Gram Staining
- Used to differentiate bacteria (unique method for bacteria)
- ![Pasted image 20260209231246.png](/img/user/7%20-%20Images/Pasted%20image%2020260209231246.png)
- Involves a dye that is used to stain bacteria:
	- Gram-Positive Bacteria (Purple)
		- Thick peptidoglycan cell walls that absorb dye more effectively
	- Gram-Negative Bacteria (Pink)
		- Thinner peptidoglycan cell walls that repel dye
