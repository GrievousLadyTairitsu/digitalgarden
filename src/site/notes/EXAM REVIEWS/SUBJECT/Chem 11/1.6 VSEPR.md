# VSEPR
#### About VSEPR
- VSEPR stands for
	- **V**alence **S**hell **El**ectron **P**air **R**epulsion
#### Steric Numbers
- The Steric Number is = to the num of bonds and lone pairs around the central atom
	- ie. $\ce O=C=O$ has a steric number of 2
