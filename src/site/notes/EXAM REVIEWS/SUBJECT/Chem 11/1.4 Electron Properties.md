# Electron charge relationships
#### Electronegativity
- How likely a particle becomes a [-] charge.
- $\ce \Delta EN = higher EN - lower EN$ 
- ![Pasted image 20260212094135.png\|551](/img/user/7%20-%20Images/Pasted%20image%2020260212094135.png)
-  This describes $\Delta EN$ values and what kind of bond they form. Note the polar cutoffs vary depending on professor as covalent ionic $\Delta EN$ is a spectrum. 
- The periodic table trend is top right has highest EN.

- Ionic Bond

- Covalent bond
     - Interaction between two atoms as they share
