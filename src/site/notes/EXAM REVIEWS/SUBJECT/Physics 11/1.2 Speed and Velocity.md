# 1.2 Speed and Velocity
#### Speed (s)
- Scalar Unit 
- Path Distance given time ($\frac{d}{t}$)
##### Instantaneous speed (v)
- Speed at a specific point of time in a s(t) function
	- $v = \frac{ds}{dt}$
##### Average speed ($v_{av}$)
- Speed over a defined period of time 
	- $v_{av}= \frac{d}{\Delta t}$
#### Velocity 
- Vector unit
- Rate of change of displacement
##### Instantaneous Velocity ($\vec{v}$)
- Velocity at a given instant of time (derivative of $\vec{r}(t)$ function)
	- $\vec{v} = \frac{d \vec{r}}{dt}$ 
##### Average Velocity ($\vec{v_{avg}}$)
- Average velocity over a defined period of time
	- $\vec{v_{avg}} = \frac{\Delta \vec{r}}{\Delta t}$
